Our vision

Your health is our main concern here at Dazzle Studio. We use Vegan Products to strengthen your nails naturally. Our products are non-toxic -- no formaldehyde, no DBP, no toluene or camphor. The air in our salon is free of odors and harmful chemicals, since we do not specialize in artificial or acrylic nails.

/01

For your peace of mind, we use an autoclave machine to sterilize all of our implements, including nail clippers, cuticle nippers, cuticle pushers, and tweezers. Every service we offer is done using a fresh sterilized pouch of implements. To ensure that your pedicure experience is clean and healthy, we use a disposable liner in your tub which is then discarded. Similarly, we discard all used buffers, files, pumice stones, flip-flops, and toe separators, unless you choose to take them home with our compliments. For those customers getting facial or waxing treatments, please rest assured that at Dazzle Studio we never double-dip our tools.
